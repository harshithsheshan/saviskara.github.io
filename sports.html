Sports Events

Tug of War

    Each team can have 9 players.
    Misbehavior will lead to disqualification
    A Player cannot play for more than a team.
    No new players will be allowed to join after the first match
    The coordinator/Judge has the final say

Throw Ball

    Each Team will consist of 6 players, 3 subs.
    A match is of 1 set of 15 points.
    Knockout starts after each team has played with every other team in the group.
    The Umpires call is final

Gully Cricket

    This is a knockout tournament
    This game will hava a small boundary and will be played for 6 overs.
    Each team should consist of 6 players and 3 subs.
    No runs for leg byes.
    If ball hit by batsman goes out of boundary whitout touching the ground(six) he is OUT.
    Runs for extras are given

Volley Ball

    Each Team will consist of 6 players, 3 subs.
    A match is of 1 set of 15 points.
    Knockout starts after each team has played with every other team in the group.
    The Umpires call is final

Slow bike race

    Riders must stay in their lane and cannot go backwards.
    Riders cannot touch the ground with their feet or anything else on their person or bicycle.
    Any rider who goes backward, rides out of the lane or touches the ground is disqualified.
    First Prize will be awarded to the Last Place Winner.
